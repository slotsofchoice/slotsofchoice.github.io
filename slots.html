Slots Basics: RTP, Volatility & Jackpots

Even simple games have important settings that affect how they play over time.[web:73]

Concept What It Means Why It Matters
RTP (Return to Player) Long-term average percentage of total stakes paid back to players.[web:73] Higher RTP means more of your wagers are expected to return as wins over a very large sample of spins.[web:73]
Volatility Describes how often a slot pays and how large the wins tend to be.[web:73] Low volatility pays smaller wins more often; high volatility can go quiet but hit big single payouts.[web:73]
Progressive Jackpot Jackpot that grows with each bet across the network until someone wins.[web:73] Offers life-changing prizes but usually with lower base-game RTP or higher variance.[web:73]
